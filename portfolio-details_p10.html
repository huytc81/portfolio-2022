SELF PORTRAIT ARTIST

  • Medium: Adobe Illustrator
  • Size: 11' x 17'
  • Date: March, 2017

Description:

Pick a well-known designer. Then use the LIVEPAINT to reveal the value of the picture. Firstly, I used pencil to trace the details of the portrait. This step spent a lot of time. After that, I filled with Black & White based on the light source. Lastly, I added some details like hair, wrinkles.

Solution:

After I finished the basic portrait, I looked normal. I think I have to make a different, then I splitted the gradation block by block to make it look characteristic and stronger then the original picture. Unexpectedly result, the illustration looks dynamic. Finally, I added in some diagonal lines to emphasize more portrait. This is an inresting project. I learned from this project was patience and creativeness in making a different from the original.
