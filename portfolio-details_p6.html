Peaches and pot - still life drawing

  • Medium: Color pencil
  • Size: 9' x 10'
  • Date: August, 2020

Description:

I love photography, it makes me release stresses. On top of that, I love to spend time to look back all the images I took and redraw it by different mediums.

Solution:

Art first, composed objects as the right perspective. Most important was maintain the key point of the still life object and color value. Another interesting aspect was analyzing the colors and looked from color harmonies, color theory to demonstrate correctly the color.My strategy was sketch the outline and filled the dark colors. Then spending time to correct the shadows, lightings. It great methodology to train the creative mind and helped me back on the normal from stressful.
