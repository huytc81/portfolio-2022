About

Huy Tran is a digital media artist, art director, and graphic designer based in San Jose, California, who was born in 1981. Through hybrid mediations, he rethinks how old art forms could be used with new digital technology as part of his research. He looks at how language and expression, presence and immateriality, voyeurism and surveillance, urbanism and hegemony, and other things come together in everyday life, using various medium to tell stories about semiotics. 3D modeling, photography, music video direction, and research are all part of his work. He rethinks traditional ways of making art in the context of digital ways of making things using open-source software. Tran's digital art focuses on using movies and animations to look at how different parts of life are connected. Tran attended San Jose State University as a media artist in 2020.
