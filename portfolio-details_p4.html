L’OREILLE ABSOLUE DÉVOILÉE by KAtja Keller - Book cover design

  • Medium: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop
  • Size: 8' x 10'
  • Date: September, 2016

Description:

The requirement for this project looks simple, it needs the eye-catching, aesthetic and minimalistic. According to the author, Katja Keller, she needed a symbolic of music in the front page and had an implication meaning. With that requirements, I did some research to convey the critical hearing and self-experience.

Solution:

In music lines, there was three types of value which were bass, alto and treble. The bass clef note satisfied the requirement symbolic and critical idea. It looked like an human ear, an emoticon. I excited with this project because I love design something simple, but have critical meaning. Beyond that, this book has been launched on Amazon as an Ebook with 189 pages.
