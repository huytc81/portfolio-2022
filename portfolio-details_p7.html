DMB - Rebrand and UI design

  • Medium: Adobe XD, Photoshop
  • Size: Width 1920px
  • Date: September, 2018

Description:

This project require rebrand an identity and create a new website. Based on my research, “Dave Matthew Band” was a famous band, it looks mature, they have a certain amount of audience. But I want to expand their music style and attract more young audience to listen.

Solution:

To find the niche of this band, I had to look their photos on social networking, watched their performances and listened to their music. I find the major point that could remake their identity and website as well. The colorful spotlight made me think it would be a figure and it was conveyed in the identity and the website. I worked on this project with fully eager of young man for a mature band and I could introduce a new visual to new segment audience.
