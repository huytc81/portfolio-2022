SELF PORTRAIT ARTIST

  • Medium: Adobe Photoshop
  • Size: 8' x 10'
  • Date: May, 2017

Description:

In this project, the leader require to design a label for oil essential which convey the natural made, charming, eco-friendly, simple to engage the consumer to buy this product. The visual had to have lavender figure, purple color that match the lavender scheme and had to be premium look.

Solution:

The client provided the specification bottle. Based on that, I created photos library with lavender flower. I came up with three options with variant designs. Lastly, I tried to mockup on the bottle to make look real. If I have to choose which one is the best, it is hard to select. I would love to mass production all of three design.
