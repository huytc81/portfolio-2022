SELF PORTRAIT ARTIST

  • Medium: Adobe Photoshop
  • Size: 8' x 10'
  • Date: May, 2017

Description:

Plastic has been using everywhere, but the enormous issue is the balancing produce and reuse. According to this problem, I want to create a poster to gain the awareness in public by the firmly message “say NO to PLASTIC”. I hope it can be go viral, impact to everyone and preserve our environment.

Solution:

To convey the I had to do many research. At first, this topic was broad, I had to narrow down and focused on plastic bag problem. Then, I looked for some images and created a moodboard to make my audience had a main point. Because, the plastic bag was the main object, so I tried to used plastic bag and shaped it into letters to make sense the idea. I was so happy with poster. In the future, I love to use many mediums to amplify it broadly.
